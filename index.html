Night Sky & Telescope Viewing Show

Welcome to the Hawkins Observatory where you'll be taken on a guided tour of the heavens. We offer a unique presentation to introduce you to the wonders of the cosmos and get you framiliar with the night sky before you'll have the opportunity to view many wonderful celestial objects though our variety of telescopes. Our guides have many years worth of knowledge and experience and can answer any of your questions about the universe and our place in it.
